About E-Waste Facility Locator

E-Waste Facility Locator is dedicated to addressing the growing concern of electronic waste (e-waste) by providing a platform that facilitates the proper disposal and recycling of electronic devices. Our mission is to create a sustainable future by encouraging responsible e-waste management.

Our Mission

At E-Waste Facility Locator, our mission is to:

  • Promote awareness about the environmental impact of e-waste.
  • Facilitate responsible recycling and disposal of electronic devices.
  • Support environmental sustainability through proper e-waste management practices.

What We Do

Our platform offers various services to achieve our mission:

  • Provide information and resources on proper e-waste disposal methods.
  • Connect individuals and businesses with certified e-waste recycling facilities.
  • Organize e-waste collection events in collaboration with local communities.

Why E-waste matters

  • Electronic waste is one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally.
  • improper disposal can lead to severe environmental and health hazards.
  • By recycling your old electronics and supporting responsible e-waste management.
  • you play a vital role in reducing pollution, conserving resources, and protecting our planet for future generations.
